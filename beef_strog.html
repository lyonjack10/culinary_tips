Beef Stroganoff



Ingrediants


  • Fresh tyhme
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • half white or yellow onion
  • Mushrooms(white, bella, skitaki)
  • 2-3 Garlic Cloves
  • Sour cream
  • Horseradish
  • Worchestire sauce
  • Butter
  • 2 or more 8 ounce steaks
  • Egg Noodles
  • Optional Vermouth

Preparation


  1. To start cook your steak in a skillet on medium to high heat until you reach your preference. Add just a little oil to the pan so that the steak does not burn, but do not drown the steak. After it is done cooking, put it to the side and cover with foil.
  2. Next roughly chop your onion and mushrooms and toss them into the skillet you cooked your meant in on medium heat. Optionally you can add butter or oil. Just don't add too much because a lot of liquid will be introduced into the dish.
  3. After you chop your vegetables go ahead and finely chop your thyme and parsley and place to the side.
  4. Finely chop your garlic and add to the skillet, wait until the garlic becomes fragrant and then mix throughly with the onions and mushrooms
  5. Now you can intoduce your liquds. Pour both half a cup of heavy cream and beef broth into the skillet. Simmer for about 15 minutes until the sauce thickens. At this time you can add your egg noodles to boiling water. Both the sauce and noodles should be done about the same time.
  6. After about 10 minutes add a tablespoon of horsradish, a few teaspoons of worchestire sauce, and a tablespoon of sour creeam to the skillet. Mix throughly. At this time you can also add a tablespoon of Vermouth if you would like. Next, take your thyme and add it to the skillet.
  7. At this time, uncover your steaks, and cut them to your desired size. Once the egg noodles are cooked at them to the skillet, mix, and then add your steak and fresh parsley on top.
